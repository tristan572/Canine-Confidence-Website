Solving the "Apartment Dog" Challenges
Living in a unit or townhouse near Westfield Chermside requires a specific set of skills. We help you address:
• Elevator & Hallway Etiquette: Transforming reactive lunging or fearful "pancaking" into calm neutrality when meeting neighbours in confined spaces.

• The "Urban On-Switch": Teaching your dog to ignore the constant distractions of buses, sirens, and heavy foot traffic so you can enjoy your local coffee run.
